Privacy Policy for Max Out in 30 Days

Effective Date: November 4, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Max Out in 30 Days! This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience.

2. Information We Collect

We collect certain personal information to provide you with a customized experience and recommended exercise plans.

  • Personal Information: We collect your email and password when you register for an account using Firebase Authentication.
  • Sensitive Information: Our app collects information regarding your health via a multiple-choice quiz. This data is used solely to personalize exercise recommendations.

3. How We Collect Information

Information is collected through:

  • A multiple-choice quiz to assess your health and exercise preferences.
  • Registration forms where you provide your email and password.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect this data to recommend an exercise plan that suits your fitness and health needs. This helps us provide a better, more personalized experience.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third-party services. Your information is used solely within the app to improve and personalize your experience.

6. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. All information is stored securely using Firebase, which applies robust security measures to protect your personal and sensitive data.

7. User Rights

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
